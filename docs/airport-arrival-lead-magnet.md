## First-Hour Arrival Worksheet

Before flying:
- Install or prepare eSIM.
- Save hotel address offline.
- Screenshot hotel check-in instructions.
- Choose primary transfer route.
- Choose backup transfer route.
- Confirm hotel late check-in.
- Decide luggage carry / deliver / transfer.

At the airport:
- Turn on eSIM or connect to Wi-Fi.
- Check immigration and baggage timing.
- Find ticket counter / QR redemption if needed.
- Confirm platform, bus stop, or pickup point.
- Message hotel if delayed.

Before leaving the airport:
- Confirm destination station or hotel area.
- Confirm last train/bus if late.
- Keep passport, tickets, wallet, phone, and battery accessible.

## Luggage Decision Grid

Carry luggage if:
- Hotel is near the arrival station.
- Route has few transfers.
- Bags are manageable.

Use luggage delivery if:
- Check-in time is later.
- You want to sightsee before hotel.
- Hotel can receive bags and timing works.

Use bus if:
- It stops near your hotel area.
- You prefer fewer transfers.
- Travel time is acceptable.

Use private transfer if:
- Arrival is late.
- Group has children or older travelers.
- Bags are large.
- Hotel route is difficult after a long flight.

## Hotel Message Template

English:
Hello,

I will arrive in Japan on [date] and may arrive at the hotel around [time].

Could you please confirm:
- Is late check-in possible?
- What should I do if I arrive after the front desk closes?
- Can the hotel receive luggage delivery before check-in?

Thank you.

Japanese:
[日付]に日本へ到着し、ホテル到着は[時間]ごろになる可能性があります。

以下について確認させてください。
・遅い時間のチェックインは可能ですか？
・フロントが閉まった後に到着する場合、どうすればよいですか？
・チェックイン前に荷物配送を受け取ってもらうことは可能ですか？

よろしくお願いいたします。
