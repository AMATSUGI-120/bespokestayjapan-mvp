## Day Plan Template

Before arrival:
- Ticket type and entry time.
- Nearest station or transfer.
- Locker or luggage plan.
- Weather plan.
- First restroom/rest stop.

First two hours:
- Priority attractions or exhibits.
- Timed entry windows.
- Family/stroller plan.
- Backup if the line is too long.

Lunch:
- Main food option.
- Dietary backup.
- Convenience store or nearby backup if re-entry is possible.

Afternoon:
- Rest area.
- Indoor backup.
- Exit time target.

After closing:
- Station route.
- Last train/bus awareness.
- Taxi/private transfer backup.
- Luggage retrieval plan.
