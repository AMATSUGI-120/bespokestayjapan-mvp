## Activity Type Filter

Snow play:
- Best for first-timers and families who want a lighter day.
- Check warm clothing, toilets, rest areas, and transport time.

Group ski day:
- Best when transport and rental logistics are bundled.
- Check lesson language, rental details, and return timing.

Private lesson:
- Best for beginners, children, and mixed ability groups.
- Check instructor language, meeting point, slope level, and cancellation.

Private transfer / car:
- Best when the route or gear makes public transport difficult.
- Check vehicle capacity, snow-road conditions, pickup, and return time.

## Packing and Rental Grid

Check whether included, rented separately, or self-provided:
- Jacket
- Snow pants
- Gloves
- Hat
- Goggles
- Boots
- Skis / snowboard
- Helmet
- Socks
- Base layers
- Hand warmers
- Change of clothes
- Towel
- Waterproof bag
- Travel insurance details

## Questions Before Booking

- Are complete beginner participants accepted?
- Is the lesson in English or another language we understand?
- Are snowwear, gloves, goggles, boots, and helmet included?
- Are lockers available for bags?
- Can we bring suitcases or should we avoid them?
- Are there indoor warm-up spaces and toilets nearby?
- What happens if the weather is poor?
- What is the cancellation or refund policy?
- Are there age, height, health, or injury restrictions?

## Review-Reading Clues
